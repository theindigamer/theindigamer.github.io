Jul 15 2017 Making numbers quickly in Piet

Piet is a stack based es­o­ter­ic pro­gram­ming lan­guage with a rel­a­tive­ly sim­ple set of in­struc­tions. At­tempt­ing to make ar­bi­trar­i­ly large num­bers us­ing as few op­er­a­tions as pos­si­ble is an in­ter­est­ing prob­lem in this con­text. We can use dy­nam­ic pro­gram­ming – in the form of depth-first search + mem­o­iza­tion – to ob­tain these op­ti­mal in­struc­tion se­quences.
